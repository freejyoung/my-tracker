🏆 The Golden Rule

Always scan your physical badge at the company terminal FIRST. This app is only for your personal tracking.

Workflow • Clock-In: Records start time.
• Meal Start/End: Tracks 30-min break.
• Clock-Out: Finalizes shift record.
Alerts • Red Blink: Meal due (4h 30m) or Finish due (7h 50m).
• Green Blink: 30-min break completed.
Settings • Popup: Toggle warning screen.
• Sound: Toggle soft alert melodies.
• New Log: Resets all data for a new day.
