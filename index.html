User Manual
🏆 The Golden Rule

Always scan your physical badge at the terminal FIRST. This app is a personal time-tracking assistant.

Workflow • Clock-In: Records your work start time.
• Meal Start/End: Tracks your 30-min lunch break.
• Clock-Out: Finalizes your log for the day.
Smart Alerts • Red Blink: After 4h 30m before lunch or 7h 50m before clock-out.
• Green Blink: After 30 minutes of meal time to signal return.
Features • Highlight: Active timer expands automatically.
• Popup/Sound: Toggle alerts in top settings.
• New Log: Resets all data after Clock-Out.
