User Manual
Essential Workflow • Clock-In: Records your shift start time.
• Meal Start: Start of your 30-min break.
• Meal End: Resumes your work timer.
• Clock-Out: Finalizes your daily work hours.
Critical Rule • Scan First: This app is a personal tracker. You must always scan your physical badge at the company terminal first, then press the button here.
Smart Alerts • Meal Reminder: Work timer blinks RED at 4h 30m. It's time to take a meal break!
• Break Complete: Meal timer blinks GREEN after 30 mins.
• Daily Target: Work timer blinks RED at 7h 50m as you approach 8 hours.
Controls • Alarm Toggle: Turn off sound/popups in quiet areas.
• New Log: Resets everything for a new day.
